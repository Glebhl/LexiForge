Glosium teaches Standard German (de_DE), so write German content in natural Standard German. German output must be real German, not learner-language text transliterated into Latin letters.

Create one fill-in-the-blank task for Glosium, a German-learning app.

Input:
- DESCRIPTION: target units, task intent, and constraints
- MODE: "word-bank" or "typing"
- LEARNER_LANGUAGE
- LEARNER_LEVEL

The learner fills blanks with German answers. In word-bank mode, the app adds the correct answers to the bank and then adds your distractors.

Requirements:
- Write 1-2 German sentences, or 3 very short German sentences.
- Use every focus unit named in DESCRIPTION unless it asks for a smaller subset.
- Every blank must test exactly one German target form: a lexeme, fixed expression, article, pronoun, verb form, preposition, adjective form, case-marked form, word-order element, or short grammar pattern.
- The hidden answer must be the thing being practiced, not a clue word around it.
- Make each blank solvable from local context, not only from the word bank.
- Provide clear clues through action, relation, collocation, time reference, contrast, or grammar need.
- Avoid vague contexts where several common words would fit.
- Do not use learner-language words or ask for translations.

Bug prevention:
- If the target is noun article, gender, or case marking, blank only the article or marked form, not the noun or full sentence.
- Do not create a blank whose correct answer is empty/no word; use another exercise type for recognition.
- If the target is a preposition, blank only the preposition.
- If the target is a verb form or auxiliary, make the time, person, number, and sentence context clear enough.

Distractors:
- Return 2-4 extra German distractors.
- Do not include correct answers again in distractors.
- Distractors must be clearly wrong for every blank by meaning, usage, or grammar.
- Avoid near-synonyms, same-category alternatives, close collocations, or forms that could still sound natural.

Response fields:
- paragraph: German text with answers marked in square brackets
- distractors: extra German distractors
