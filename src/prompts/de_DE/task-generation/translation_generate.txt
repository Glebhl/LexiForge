Glosium teaches Standard German (de_DE), so write German content in natural Standard German. German output must be real German, not learner-language text transliterated into Latin letters.

Create one translation task for Glosium, a German-learning app.

Input:
- DESCRIPTION: target units, direction, task intent, and constraints
- MODE: "word-bank" or "typing"
- LEARNER_LANGUAGE: source language for the learner prompt
- LEARNER_LEVEL

The learner must always translate into German. This is true for both word-bank and typing modes.

Requirements:
- Write the source text entirely in LEARNER_LANGUAGE.
- Write all acceptable answers as full German translations.
- Do not create German answers by romanizing or transliterating LEARNER_LANGUAGE words.
- Use every focus unit named in DESCRIPTION in the intended German answer set.
- For vocabulary, cue the named German lexeme, phrase, collocation, or fixed expression through concrete learner-language meaning.
- For grammar, cue the required German form through clear meaning, time, and context.
- Use 1-2 very short source sentences, or 3 extremely short ones.
- Every source sentence must correspond to at least one required German focus unit.
- Keep meaning concrete, literal, and unambiguous.
- Avoid prompts that allow many equally natural translations, near-synonyms, or broad paraphrases.
- Provide all truly acceptable full-sentence answers, but do not duplicate answers that differ only by punctuation or minor spelling variants.

Bug prevention:
- Never make the learner translate from German into LEARNER_LANGUAGE.
- Never make typing require an answer in LEARNER_LANGUAGE.
- Do not mix languages inside one field.
- Do not show the target German word in the source text.
- If the focus is articles, prepositions, verb forms, or another small form, make the German form necessary, not optional.

Distractors:
- Return 3-5 extra German distractor words or short chunks for the word bank.
- Distractors are not full translations.
- Do not include words already required by the correct answers.
- Avoid near-synonyms, same-category alternatives, or words that could create another valid translation.

Response fields:
- paragraph: source text in LEARNER_LANGUAGE
- answers: full German translations
- distractors: extra German distractor words or short chunks
