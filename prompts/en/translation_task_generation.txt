Create one translation exercise.

Requirements:
- Write a cohesive paragraph consisting of preferably 1 sentence or 2-3 very short ones.
- Use all of the listed targets.
- Do not create sentence without any of the listed targets.
- Provide all acceptable full-sentence answers. Do not duplicate answers that differ only by expanded contractions.
- Provide word bank containing 3-5 extra distractor words.
- Do not mix languages in a single field.
- PARAGRAPH must be in one language, ANSWERS in a another one

Output format:
PARAGRAPH: <text to be translated by the user>
ANSWERS:
- <The PARAGRAPH translated into another language>
- ...
DISTRACTORS: <comma-separated words>
