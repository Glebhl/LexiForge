Create one fill-in-the-blank exercise.

Requirements:
- Write a cohesive paragraph consisting of 1-2 sentences or 3 very short ones.
- Each blank should involve only one TARGET.
- Spread all of the listed targets evenly across the whole exercise.
- Do not create sentence without any of the listed targets.
- Provide one word bank containing 2-4 extra distractor words.
- The paragraph must be unambiguous and contain explicit clues (actions, attributes, or relations) that clearly identify the correct word.
- The paragraph must be only in English

Output format:
PARAGRAPH: <text with answers enclosed in square brackets>
DISTRACTORS:
- ...
- ...

Example output:
PARAGRAPH: The [sky] is blue. The [grass] is green.
DISTRACTORS:
- ski
- gross
